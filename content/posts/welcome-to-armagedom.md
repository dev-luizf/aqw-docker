Armagedom Worlds has a new portal.

This rebuild keeps the world and its history intact while giving the website a
smaller, safer foundation. News will live here as Markdown dispatches, the game
remains available directly in the browser, and account tools no longer depend
on the old portal.

## What remains

- The Spider game client and its familiar world.
- Existing maps, monsters, items, shops, and quests.
- The SmartFox game server and browser socket bridge.

## What changes

The portal is now deliberately focused: news, play, and account security. That
means less old machinery to maintain and more room to improve the game itself.
